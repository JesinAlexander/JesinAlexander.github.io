About Us

This is a ‘Baby Care and Parenthood Information Website’.
This website is intended for all the new moms and dads and also to be parents who has several queries and worries for their little ones,
and many a time they cannot contact paediatricians/gynecologist for their all-small queries.
This is an informative website which provides all the basic information regarding parenting,it is an open website which welcomes all to
share their view and blogs and their experience making parenthood journey for other parents easy.
This site has the facility to post any queries and any one can provide you the answers based on their experience. Being a new mom,
I do understand the need of such platforms where moms can share their view and talk about their concer.
Its a emotional phase of one life and they go through lot of hormonal changes and moods.
With that intention we have created this website it is not-for-profit but it is a web service providing free and confidential help.
The information we provide is not intended to be a substitute for anyone requiring medical advice or professional counselling.
We hope that you will continue to explore our website and access information, resources and the caring support of our nationwide network
and use it for the intention for which this site is created and provide us the feedback and wishes to improve the website with more
features and more professional help.
